Companion · interaction spec

Every intervention, on one page.

Modes, animations, voice, page behaviors, concierge logic, and what the agent knows. The companion is live on the bottom right — press Space to talk, click the mascot to follow.

Companion modes

  • Voice copilot — always-on voice concierge. Cues, talks, suggests as you browse.
  • Cursor copilot — follows your mouse, drops contextual nudges near the cursor.
  • Yoda copilot — observes silently, builds a profile, then follows with smarter suggestions.
  • Dock + move — docked like a bot icon, detaches and follows when you engage.
  • Studio — side panel for deeper interactions (try-on, comparisons, bundles). Opens from companion CTA.

Companion animations

  • Vacuum absorb — particles flow from product images, text, prices into the companion. "I'm reading the page."
  • Squiggly thinking — dotted lines trace around the companion, connecting and rearranging. Eyes narrow.
  • Particle burst — compressed particles explode outward softly when response is ready.
  • Eye tracking — pupils follow cursor with a lazy delay.
  • Blink states — slow (idle), double (attention), wide (about to speak), squint (thinking).
  • Breathing glow — ambient pulse, intensity rises when there's something to say.
  • Product orbit — 4–6 product thumbnails orbit the companion. Tappable. Companion looks at the recommended one.

Voice interactions

  • Hold Space (desktop) or tap mic (mobile) to speak.
  • Wave bars — five vertical bars pulse with audio amplitude while listening.
  • Connecting dots — key terms appear and connect ("crystals" — "under $5" — "warm tones"), then absorb into the companion.
  • Voice ring — expanding ripple pulses outward when it speaks back.
  • Multi-turn context — "show me crystals under $5" → "now just the warm ones" works without re-explaining.

Page-level interactions

  • Grid morph — non-matching products shrink and fade, matches scale up and rearrange.
  • Dynamic page generation — voice query produces a curated page: "Curated for you: Desk Crystals, Warm Tones, Under $5".
  • Card highlight — subtle glow on product cards when the companion detects hesitation.
  • Inline badge — contextual tags fade in: "Lowest in 30 days" or "Runs small".

Concierge behaviors

  • Proactive greeting — "I see 38 products here. What are you looking for?"
  • Context questions — "Where are you putting this?" → "What color is your desk?"
  • Color/room matching — "Yellow pops against white. For subtle, try the selenite."
  • Memory offer — "Save this so I can suggest similar things as you browse?"
  • Cross-category connection — "Crystals + cushions — decorating a room? Here's a mood board."
  • Bundle builder — "Put these together as a set? Total: $24.50."
  • Cart threshold nudge — "$8 from free shipping. Want a small add-on?"
  • Upsell with context — only after trust: "Jade pagoda at $19.90 is the statement piece."

Knowledge base

  • Knows — full catalog (names, prices, colors, sizes, materials, availability), brand info, shipping, returns, FAQs, review summaries, size/fit data.
  • Doesn't reveal — sales numbers, internal margins, inventory counts, revenue.

Positioning

  • Mid-right (default) — vertically centered, doesn't overlap content.
  • Logo emerge — slides out of header logo on activation, returns on dismiss.
  • Bottom-right dock — familiar bot position, for conservative brands.
