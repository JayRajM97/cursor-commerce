CSV-generated storefront

Raw product data, turned into a live shopping surface.

Phase one converts the SHEIN and Walmart CSVs into a curated marketplace. The first demo leans into SHEIN crystals and home decor, then uses Walmart for review-rich support categories.

The concierge layer will sit on top of this catalog.

Product opens, saves, color switches, and long dwell time become signals for what the agent should ask or recommend next.

Opened yellow crystal -> ask desk, shelf, or gifting Desk intent -> ask room and surface color Dark wood + white room -> suggest jade, blue, or selenite
